<h1>Social Media Do's and Don'ts </h1>  
<h2>Do's</h2>
<ul>
    <li>Use strong, unique passwords for all accounts.</li>
    <li>Think before posting anything publicly.</li>
    <li>Keep your social media profiles private whenever possible.</li>
    <li>Verify information before sharing it online.</li>
    <li>Be respectful and polite in all online interactions.</li>
</ul>

<h2>Don'ts</h2>
<ul>
    <li>Don’t share personal details like OTP, passwords, or bank information.</li>
    <li>Don’t post your location or travel plans publicly.</li>
    <li>Don’t accept friend requests from unknown people.</li>
    <li>Don’t share fake news or unverified content.</li>
    <li>Don’t engage in arguments, cyberbullying, or negative comments.</li>
</ul>
