
<h1>Ransomware Prevention Checklist</h1>

<ul>
    <li>Avoid clicking unknown links or attachments in emails.</li>
    <li>Do not download software or files from untrusted websites.</li>
    <li>Keep your laptop and mobile device updated with the latest security patches.</li>
    <li>Install antivirus software and run regular security scans.</li>
    <li>Backup important files regularly to avoid data loss.</li>
    <li>Never share your OTP, UPI PIN, banking details, or personal information.</li>
    <li>Always double-check UPI IDs and payment links before sending money.</li>
    <li>Use strong passwords and enable two-factor authentication on all accounts.</li>
    <li>Avoid using public WiFi for financial transactions or important logins.</li>
    <li>Report cyber incidents to:</li>
</ul>

<p>

Cyber Crime Helpline: <strong>1930</strong>
